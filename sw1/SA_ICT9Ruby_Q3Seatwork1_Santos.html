My favorite reality TV show


The Synopsis

The big bang theory show revolves around some of the misadventures of two physicists who work at the California Institute of Technology, Leonard Hofstadter and Sheldon Cooper. expanding out from these co-workers and roommates to include their fellow Caltech scientists, neighbors in their building and lovers.

The Characters:

Sheldon Cooper

The main Character of The Big Bang Theory. Being known for being a theoretical genius but also his eccentric behavour. He has a hard time understanding what sarcasm is, with the exception of his close friends and wife Amy, uncomfortable with many social situations.

Leonard Hofstadter

Leonard Hofstadter is an experimental physicist at Caltech in Pasadena. Although Leonard has many geeky interests and hobbies, he was the most willing of the guys to try and socialize with other people.

Penny Hofstadter

Penny Hofstadter is a pharmaceutical sales rep for Zangen Pharmaceuticals, who formerly worked as a waitress at the Cheesecake Factory.

Raj Koothrappali

an Indian-born astrophysicist who lives in Pasadena and works at Caltech. Raj now also presents shows at the Griffith Planetarium. For a long time, Raj was unable to talk to women due to a case of selective mutism. Raj usually needed a beer in his hand before he could even talk to women, including his close friends.

Howard Wolowitz

is an aerospace engineer and former NASA astronaut who works at Caltech in Pasadena. Howard is married to Bernadette Rostenkowski-Wolowitz, with whom he has a daughter, Halley, and a son, Neil Michael. Howard is best friends with Raj Koothrappali.

Amy Farrah Fowler

Dr. Amy Farrah Fowler, Ph.D. is a neurobiologist from Glendale, California, who is married to Dr. Sheldon Cooper. Amy met Sheldon Cooper after Howard and Raj created a fake dating profile for him, and Amy's profile was matched to his.

Bernadette Rostenkowski-Wolowitz

Bernadette Rostenkowski-Wolowitz is a microbiologist who has a high-paying job at a pharmaceuticals company, Zangen. She is married to Howard Wolowitz, with whom she has two children, Halley and Neil Michael.
